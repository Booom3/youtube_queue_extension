  • 1. If the video is currently playing Double right click on it, else Right click on a youtube video thumbnail to add video to the queue. .
  • 2. Queue is dynamic and is independent of the Playlists.
  • 3. Click on any video in Queue to start playing the videos from that point.
  • 4. Click on '+' icon of any video in queue and select the playlist you want the video to be added to.
  • 5. Go to 'Playlists' tab to manage playlists.
  • 6. Click on "add playlist" to add an empty playlist.
  • 7. Click on any Playlist to play it and load the songs to the queue.
  • 8. Click on Loop icon to toggle it. If turned on, it repeats all the songs in the queue.
  • 9. Click on Empty icon to clear the queue.
